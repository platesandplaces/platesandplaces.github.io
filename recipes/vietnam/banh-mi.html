Bánh mì

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Vietnam | Type: Main

A crusty baguette filled with savoury meats, pickled vegetables and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Vietnamese or French baguette
  • Protein (grilled pork, chicken or pâté)
  • Pickled carrots and daikon
  • Cucumber slices
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Jalapeño or chilli slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Soy sauce or Maggi seasoning

Method

  1. Prepare the pickled carrots and daikon by soaking them in vinegar and sugar.
  2. Slice and lightly toast the baguette; spread mayonnaise on both sides.
  3. Layer the protein, pickled vegetables, cucumber, cilantro and chilli.
  4. Finish with a drizzle of soy sauce or Maggi seasoning.
