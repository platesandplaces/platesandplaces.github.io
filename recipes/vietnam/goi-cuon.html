Gỏi cuốn (Fresh Spring Rolls)

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Vietnam | Type: Snack

Fresh rice paper rolls filled with shrimp, pork, vermicelli noodles and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Vermicelli noodles, cooked
  • Cooked shrimp, halved
  • Cooked pork slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Fresh mint and cilantro
  • Dipping sauce (hoisin-peanut or nuoc cham)

Method

  1. Briefly dip a rice paper wrapper into warm water until pliable.
  2. Place fillings in the center: lettuce, herbs, noodles, shrimp and pork.
  3. Fold in the sides and roll tightly to enclose the fillings.
  4. Serve with your preferred dipping sauce.
