Tomato Egg Drop Soup (Canh Cà Chua Trứng)

Vietnam · Soup

Light, homestyle Vietnamese soup with tomato, egg ribbons, and dried shrimp umami.

Serves: 4 · Prep: 5 minutes · Wait: 0 minutes · Cook: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Broth Base

  • 1/4 cup dried shrimp (tôm khô)
  • 1/2 small brown onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium roma tomatoes, deseeded and quartered
  • 1.2L water
  • 1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (to taste)

Soup Additions

  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper

Instructions

  1. Soak dried shrimp 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Sauté onion, add shrimp 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes 2–3 minutes; pour water and simmer 5–7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and fish sauce. Swirl in beaten eggs to form ribbons; simmer 30 seconds.
  5. Add spring onions and white pepper; serve hot.

Notes

  • Vegetarian: omit dried shrimp; use mushroom seasoning or veg broth.
  • Pouring while stirring makes delicate ribbons.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 2 days; reheat gently (avoid boiling to preserve egg texture).

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve alongside rice and a savoury main.
