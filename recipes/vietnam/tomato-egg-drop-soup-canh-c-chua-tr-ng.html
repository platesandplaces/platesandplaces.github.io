Tomato Egg Drop Soup (Canh Cà Chua Trứng)

Vietnam • Soup • Serves 4 • Prep 5 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 15 minutes

Light home-style soup with tomato, egg ribbons, and dried-shrimp umami.

Ingredients

Broth Base

  • 1/4 cup dried shrimp
  • 1/2 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 roma tomatoes, deseeded and quartered
  • 1.2L water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fish sauce

Soup Additions

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

Instructions

  1. Soak dried shrimp 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Sauté onion; add shrimp 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes; cook 2–3 minutes. Add water; simmer 5–7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and fish sauce. Swirl in beaten eggs; simmer 30 seconds.
  5. Finish with spring onion and pepper.

Notes

  • Vegetarian: omit dried shrimp; use mushroom seasoning.
  • Pour egg while stirring for ribbons.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 2 days; reheat gently (don’t boil).

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with rice and a savoury main.
