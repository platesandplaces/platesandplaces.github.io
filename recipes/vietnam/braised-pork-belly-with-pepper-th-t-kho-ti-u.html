Braised Pork Belly with Pepper (Thịt Kho Tiêu)

Vietnam • Main • Serves 2–3 • Prep 10 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 45 minutes

Peppery, savoury‑sweet clay‑pot style braise.

Ingredients

Pork Belly

  • 500g pork belly, bite‑size
  • 2 shallots, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2–3 red chillies (optional)
  • 2 green onions (whites chunked; greens chopped)

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp light soy
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1 tsp seasoning powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp nước màu
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Brown pork; remove excess fat.
  2. Sauté shallot/garlic; return pork.
  3. Add sauce and chillies; cover and simmer 30 min.
  4. Uncover; reduce to glossy coating. Finish with extra pepper and scallion greens.

Notes

  • Balanced fat/meat belly works best.
  • Nước màu adds colour/sweetness.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; flavours deepen overnight.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with rice and quick pickles.
