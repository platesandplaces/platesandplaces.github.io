Fried Rice with Chinese Sausage (Cơm Chiên Lạp Xưởng)

Vietnam • Rice • Serves 4 • Prep 15 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 10 minutes

Fragrant fried rice with savoury-sweet lạp xưởng; fast and satisfying.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 3 Chinese sausages, diced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion (~140g), diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 4 spring onions (greens/whites separated)
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 500g cooked day-old white rice

Seasoning

  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Brown sausage; push aside.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic; add veg and spring-onion whites.
  3. Scramble eggs; mix through.
  4. Add rice; season with soy, honey, garlic powder; adjust salt/pepper.
  5. Stir in spring-onion greens; serve.

Notes

  • Day‑old rice separates best.
  • Steam lạp xưởng briefly before slicing (optional).

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; reheat in pan/microwave.

Serving Suggestions

  • Great with cucumber slices or a fried egg.
