Fried Rice with Chinese Sausage (Cơm Chiên Lạp Xưởng)

Vietnam · Rice

Vietnamese-style fried rice with savoury-sweet lạp xưởng; fast, fragrant, satisfying.

Serves: 4 · Prep: 15 minutes · Wait: 0 minutes · Cook: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 3 Chinese sausages (lạp xưởng), diced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium brown onion (~140g), diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (carrot, corn, peas)
  • 4 stalks spring onion, chopped (greens and whites separated)
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 500g cooked day-old white rice

Seasoning

  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Brown diced sausage; set aside to one side of pan.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic; add mixed veg; add spring onion whites.
  3. Scramble eggs, then mix through.
  4. Add rice (break up clumps), then soy sauce, honey, garlic powder; season to taste.
  5. Stir in spring onion greens; serve hot.

Notes

  • Day‑old rice separates better.
  • Steam lạp xưởng briefly before slicing to reduce grease (optional).

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 3 days; reheat in pan or microwave.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with cucumber slices, a fried egg, or pickles.
