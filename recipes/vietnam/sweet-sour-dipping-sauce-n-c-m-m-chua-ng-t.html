Sweet‑Sour Dipping Sauce (Nước Mắm Chua Ngọt)

Vietnam · Sauce/Condiment

Classic Vietnamese balanced fish sauce dip for rolls, grilled meats, and noodle bowls.

Serves: about 1 cup · Prep: 5 minutes · Wait: 0 minutes · Cook: 0 minutes

Ingredients

Sauce Base

  • 6 tbsp fish sauce
  • 6 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp lime juice or vinegar
  • 10 tbsp water

Flavour Add‑ins

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 red chilli, sliced

Instructions

  1. Stir fish sauce, sugar, lime/vinegar, and water until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add garlic and chilli; rest a few minutes before serving.

Notes

  • Lime = fresher taste; vinegar = longer keeping.
  • Adjust water/sugar for your fish sauce saltiness.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 1 week; stir before using.

Serving Suggestions

  • Great with bún thịt nướng, chả giò, salads, and noodle bowls.
