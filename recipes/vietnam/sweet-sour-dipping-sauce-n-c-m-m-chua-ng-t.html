Sweet‑Sour Dipping Sauce (Nước Mắm Chua Ngọt)

Vietnam • Sauce/Condiment • Serves ~1 cup • Prep 5 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 0 minutes

Balanced fish-sauce dip for rolls, grilled meats, and noodle salads.

Ingredients

Sauce Base

  • 6 tbsp fish sauce
  • 6 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp lime juice or vinegar
  • 10 tbsp water

Flavour Add‑ins

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 red chilli, sliced

Instructions

  1. Stir fish sauce, sugar, citrus, and water until dissolved.
  2. Add garlic and chilli; rest a few minutes.

Notes

  • Lime = fresher; vinegar = longer keeping.
  • Adjust water/sugar for your fish sauce saltiness.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 1 week; stir before using.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with bún thịt nướng, chả giò, salads.
