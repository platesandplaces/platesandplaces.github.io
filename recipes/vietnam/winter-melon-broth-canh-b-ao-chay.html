Winter Melon Broth (Canh Bí Đao Chay)

Vietnam • Soup • Serves 4 • Prep 10 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 15 minutes

Light, clear soup — mild, cooling, everyday staple.

Ingredients

For the Broth

  • 400g winter melon, chunks
  • 600ml water or light veg broth
  • 1 small shallot, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped

Seasoning

  • 1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)

Garnish

  • Chopped coriander
  • Chopped spring onion

Instructions

  1. Sauté shallot and garlic; add water/broth.
  2. Add melon; simmer 10–12 minutes until translucent.
  3. Season, garnish, serve.

Notes

  • Blanch/freeze melon for convenience.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3–4 days; reheat gently.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with rice and a salty side.
