Scallion Oil (Mỡ Hành)

Vietnam • Condiment • Serves ~1 cup • Prep 5 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 5 minutes

Hot oil over chopped spring onions; a go‑to finishing condiment.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 4 stalks spring onion, finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp neutral oil
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Place chopped spring onion in heatproof bowl.
  2. Heat oil until shimmering; pour over.
  3. Add salt; cool and store.

Notes

  • Optional: splash fish sauce or pinch of sugar.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 5 days; bring to room temp.

Serving Suggestions

  • Great on cơm tấm, grilled meats, noodles.
