Scallion Oil (Mỡ Hành)

Vietnam · Condiment

Fragrant hot oil over chopped spring onions; a go‑to finishing condiment.

Serves: Approx. 1 cup · Prep: 5 minutes · Wait: 0 minutes · Cook: 5 minutes

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 4 stalks spring onion, finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp neutral oil
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Place chopped spring onions in a heatproof bowl.
  2. Heat oil until shimmering; pour over onions.
  3. Add pinch of salt; cool and store.

Notes

  • Optional: a splash of fish sauce or a pinch of sugar.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 5 days; bring to room temp before using.

Serving Suggestions

  • Drizzle over grilled meats, rice, noodles; great on cơm tấm.
