Country: Vietnam | Type: Dessert

A layered dessert of coloured beans, jelly and coconut milk over ice.

Ingredients

  • Sweet red beans
  • Sweet mung beans
  • Pandan jelly, cut into cubes
  • Coconut milk
  • Sugar
  • Crushed ice

Method

  1. Cook the red beans and mung beans separately until tender and lightly sweetened.
  2. Prepare the pandan jelly and cut into small cubes.
  3. Layer the red beans, mung beans and pandan jelly in a tall glass.
  4. Add crushed ice and pour sweetened coconut milk over the top.
  5. Serve with a spoon and enjoy the layers of flavours and textures.
