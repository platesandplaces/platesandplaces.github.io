Bún bò Nam Bộ

Country: Vietnam | Type: Salad

A warm salad of rice noodles topped with sautéed beef, herbs and a nuoc cham dressing.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Onion, sliced
  • Lettuce and fresh herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Fried shallots and peanuts
  • Nuoc cham dressing (fish sauce, lime juice, sugar)

Method

  1. Cook the rice noodles and rinse with cold water; drain well.
  2. Sauté the beef with garlic and onions until just cooked.
  3. Arrange lettuce and herbs in bowls, top with noodles and beef.
  4. Drizzle with nuoc cham dressing and garnish with fried shallots and peanuts.
