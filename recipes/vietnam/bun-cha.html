Country: Vietnam | Type: Main

A Hanoi specialty of grilled pork served with vermicelli noodles, fresh herbs and dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 300 g ground pork
  • 300 g pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 200 g vermicelli noodles
  • Fresh herbs (lettuce, mint, perilla)
  • Dipping sauce (nuoc cham)

Method

  1. Season the ground pork and pork belly with fish sauce, sugar, garlic and shallot; shape ground pork into patties.
  2. Grill the pork patties and pork belly until caramelised and cooked through.
  3. Cook vermicelli noodles and rinse under cold water.
  4. Serve pork with noodles, fresh herbs and nuoc cham for dipping.
