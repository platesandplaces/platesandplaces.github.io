Country: Vietnam | Type: Soup

A fragrant beef noodle soup with slow-simmered broth, rice noodles and tender slices of beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef bones
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 1 piece ginger, halved
  • 3 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 250 g beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 200 g rice noodles
  • Bean sprouts, Thai basil, cilantro and lime wedges

Method

  1. Char the onion and ginger over an open flame or under a broiler.
  2. Simmer beef bones with charred onion, ginger, spices and fish sauce for 4–6 hours; skim as needed.
  3. Strain the broth and keep it hot.
  4. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Place noodles in bowls, top with raw beef slices and pour hot broth over to cook the beef.
  6. Serve with bean sprouts, herbs and lime wedges.
