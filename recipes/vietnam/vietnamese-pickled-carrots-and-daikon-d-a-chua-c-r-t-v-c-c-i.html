Vietnamese Pickled Carrots and Daikon (Dưa Chua Cà Rốt và Củ Cải)

Vietnam • Condiment • Serves 4 cups (approx. 960ml) • Prep 15 minutes • Wait 2 hours • Cook 0 minutes

Staple Vietnamese pickle for bánh mì, noodle bowls, and grilled meats.

Ingredients

For the Pickled Vegetables

  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into 3mm batons (Note 1)
  • 1/2 large daikon, peeled and cut into 3mm batons (Note 1)
  • 375ml boiling water
  • 100g white sugar
  • 20g salt
  • 180ml rice wine vinegar (Note 2)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve sugar and salt in boiling water.
  2. Stir in vinegar.
  3. Add carrot and daikon; ensure submerged.
  4. Rest 2 hours.
  5. Store submerged in a jar; refrigerate (up to 2 months).

Notes

  • Cut size 3mm = even texture.
  • Rice vinegar best; ACV ok but tangier.
  • Keep veg submerged; glass jars preferred.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 2 months (submerged).

Serving Suggestions

  • Great with bánh mì, grilled meats, noodle bowls.
