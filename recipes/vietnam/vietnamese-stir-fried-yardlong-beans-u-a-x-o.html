Vietnamese Stir‑Fried Yardlong Beans (Đậu Đũa Xào)

Vietnam • Side • Serves 4 • Prep 10 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 10 minutes

Crisp‑tender beans in a savoury fish sauce‑oyster glaze.

Ingredients

Main

  • 400g yardlong beans, 5cm pieces
  • 1 tsp salt (for washing)
  • 60g red bell pepper, sliced (optional)
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp oil (for beans) + 2 tbsp (for stir‑fry)

Sauce

  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/3 tsp black pepper
  • 1/3 tsp MSG (optional)
  • 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Wash/steam beans 2 min; set aside.
  2. Mix sauce.
  3. Stir‑fry aromatics; add beans (and pepper).
  4. Add sauce; toss 1–2 min to coat.

Notes

  • Green beans substitute (shorter steam).

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 2 days; quick pan reheat.

Serving Suggestions

  • Pair with jasmine rice and pork chops.
