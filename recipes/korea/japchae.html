Country: Korea | Type: Side

Glass noodles stir-fried with colorful vegetables and sweet-savoury sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200 g sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon)
  • 200 g beef, sliced
  • Spinach, blanched
  • Carrot, julienned
  • Onion, sliced
  • Bell pepper, sliced
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Sesame seeds

Method

  1. Cook the sweet potato noodles according to package directions; rinse and cut to shorter lengths.
  2. Stir-fry the beef with garlic until browned; set aside.
  3. Stir-fry vegetables separately until tender.
  4. Combine noodles, beef and vegetables; season with soy sauce, sugar and sesame oil.
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
