Pickled White Radish (Chicken Mu, 치킨무)

Korea • Pickles • Serves ~4 cups • Prep 10 minutes • Wait 1–3 days • Cook 0 minutes

Crisp sweet‑tangy daikon cubes — classic fried‑chicken pickle.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 small–medium white radish (400–500g), 1.5 cm cubes
  • 500 ml water
  • 400 ml vinegar (grain/rice)
  • 300 ml white sugar (~260–270g)
  • 1 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cube radish in container.
  2. Dissolve water, vinegar, sugar, salt; pour to submerge.
  3. Chill 24h (best after 2–3 days).

Notes

  • Optional additions: green chilli, garlic, kombu.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 3 weeks.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve cold with KFC, grilled pork belly, or rich rice dishes.
