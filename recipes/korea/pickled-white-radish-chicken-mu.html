Pickled White Radish (Chicken Mu, 치킨무)

Korea · Pickles

Crisp sweet‑tangy Korean daikon cubes — classic with fried chicken.

Serves: Makes ~4 cups · Prep: 10 minutes · Wait: 1–3 days · Cook: 0 minutes

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 small–medium white radish (400–500g), 1.5 cm cubes
  • 500 ml water
  • 400 ml vinegar (Korean grain or rice)
  • 300 ml white sugar (~260–270g)
  • 1 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cube radish; place in container.
  2. Dissolve water, vinegar, sugar, salt. Pour over to submerge.
  3. Refrigerate 24h (best 2–3 days).

Notes

  • Additions: green chilli, garlic, or kombu for depth.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 3 weeks.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Korean fried chicken, grilled pork belly, or rich rice dishes.
