Tteokbokki

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Korea | Type: Snack

A beloved street food of rice cakes simmered in spicy-sweet sauce.

Ingredients

  • 400 g Korean rice cakes (tteok)
  • 200 g fish cakes, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp gochujang (red chili paste)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 green onion, sliced

Method

  1. Combine water, gochujang, sugar, soy sauce and garlic in a pan; bring to a boil.
  2. Add rice cakes and fish cakes; simmer until the rice cakes soften and the sauce thickens.
  3. Garnish with sliced green onion and serve hot.
