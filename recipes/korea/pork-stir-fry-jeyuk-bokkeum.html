Pork Stir‑Fry (Jeyuk Bokkeum - 제육볶음)

Korea • Main • Serves 4 • Prep 15 minutes • Wait 2+ hours (marinade) • Cook 20 minutes

Spicy‑savory pork with gochujang, garlic, and pear.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 500g pork shoulder/belly, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, half‑moons
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chillies (optional)
  • 100g cabbage or enoki (optional)

Marinade

  • 1 small Korean/nashi pear, grated (~1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 small onion, grated (~1/4 cup)
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic (3–4 cloves)
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 3 tbsp gochujang
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp doenjang (optional)
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru (optional)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp mirin or sake

To Serve

  • Rice, perilla leaves, kimchi, banchan; sesame seeds, sliced green onions

Instructions

  1. Mix marinade; coat pork; marinate 2h–overnight.
  2. Stir‑fry pork in batches; set aside.
  3. Stir‑fry veg; return pork; combine and finish.

Notes

  • Use glass/ceramic container; gochujang stains plastic.
  • Ask butcher for thin-sliced pork.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; splash water to loosen when reheating.

Serving Suggestions

  • Great with gamja jorim, dubu jorim, pickled perilla, kongnamul, oi muchim.
