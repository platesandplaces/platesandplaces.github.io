Korean Braised Peanuts (Tangkong Jorim / 땅콩조림)

Korea · Side/Snack

Lightly seasoned, crisp, dry‑fried peanuts with simple salt‑sugar finish.

Serves: 2–3 · Prep: 5 minutes · Wait: 0 minutes · Cook: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw peanuts (with skin)
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Stir‑fry peanuts in oil over medium heat 8–10 minutes until glossy and popping.
  2. Drain; toss warm with sugar and salt. Cool completely.

Notes

  • Add pinch of garlic powder or splash of soy near the end for savoury twist.

Storage & Reheating

  • Airtight jar up to 1 week.

Serving Suggestions

  • Great snack with drinks or in a Korean banchan spread.
