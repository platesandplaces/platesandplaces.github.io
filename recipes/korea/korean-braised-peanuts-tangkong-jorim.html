Korean Braised Peanuts (Tangkong Jorim / 땅콩조림)

Korea • Side/Snack • Serves 2–3 • Prep 5 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 10 minutes

Lightly seasoned, crisp, dry‑fried peanuts with salt‑sugar finish.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw peanuts (with skin)
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Stir‑fry peanuts in oil 8–10 minutes until glossy and popping.
  2. Drain; toss warm with sugar and salt; cool completely.

Notes

  • Add pinch garlic powder or splash soy for savoury twist.

Storage & Reheating

  • Airtight up to 1 week.

Serving Suggestions

  • Snack with drinks or part of banchan spread.
