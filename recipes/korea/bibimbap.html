A vibrant mixed rice bowl topped with saut√©ed vegetables, beef and spicy gochujang sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Spinach, carrot, zucchini and bean sprouts, prepared separately
  • 100 g beef, marinated and cooked
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp gochujang (chili paste)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds

Method

  1. Prepare and season each vegetable separately with sesame oil and salt.
  2. Cook the marinated beef in a pan.
  3. Fry the egg sunny-side up.
  4. Arrange rice in a bowl and top with vegetables, beef and egg.
  5. Serve with gochujang and sesame seeds.
