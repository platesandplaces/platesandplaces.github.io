White Kimchi (백김치)

Korea · Pickles

Mild kimchi without chilli flakes; sweet, tangy, refreshing.

Serves: 8–10 · Prep: 40 minutes · Wait: 2–3 days ferment · Cook: 0

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 medium napa cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sea salt
  • Water (for soaking)
  • 1 small Korean pear or apple, julienned
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 small daikon, julienned
  • 2–3 jujubes or raisins (optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 slice ginger
  • Brine: 2 cups water, 1 tbsp glutinous rice flour, 1 tsp sugar, 2 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Soak quartered cabbage in salted water 3–4h; rinse, drain.
  2. Make stuffing; layer in cabbage.
  3. Cook brine base (water + rice flour + sugar) gently; cool; add salt.
  4. Pack cabbage; pour brine. Ferment 1 day room temp, then chill 2–3 days.

Notes

  • Ferments faster in warmth; flavour mellows with age.

Storage & Reheating

  • Refrigerate 2–3 weeks.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve cold with rich or spicy dishes.
