Korean fermented Napa cabbage with chili, garlic and fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Napa cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sea salt
  • 3 tbsp Korean chili flakes (gochugaru)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce

Method

  1. Cut the cabbage into pieces and sprinkle with salt; let sit for 1-2 hours.
  2. Rinse the cabbage and drain well.
  3. Mix chili flakes, garlic, ginger, fish sauce and green onions to make a paste.
  4. Coat the cabbage with the chili paste and pack into a jar.
  5. Ferment at room temperature for 1-3 days before moving to the fridge.
