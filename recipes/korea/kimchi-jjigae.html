Kimchi Jjigae

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Korea | Type: Soup

A hearty stew combining kimchi, pork, tofu and spicy seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups kimchi, chopped
  • 200 g pork belly, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 block tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups stock (anchovy or chicken)
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru (red pepper flakes)
  • 1 tbsp gochujang (red pepper paste)
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onion for garnish

Method

  1. Saut√© kimchi and pork belly in sesame oil until fragrant.
  2. Add stock, gochugaru and gochujang; bring to a boil.
  3. Add tofu and onion; simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Garnish with chopped green onion before serving.
