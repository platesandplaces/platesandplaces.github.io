Beef Bulgogi (Traditional Korean BBQ Beef) 불고기

Korea • Main • Serves 3–4 • Prep 15 minutes • Wait 1 hour (or overnight) • Cook 6 minutes

Thin-sliced beef marinated with soy, pear, garlic, sesame — grill or pan‑sear.

Ingredients

Beef & Vegetables

  • 600–800g thinly sliced beef
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • (Optional) 1 small carrot, julienned
  • (Optional) 100g mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • Lettuce/perilla leaves to serve

Marinade

  • 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar or honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp mirin or 1 tbsp makgeolli (optional)
  • 4 tbsp grated Korean pear (or apple)
  • 2 tbsp grated onion
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix marinade; coat beef; chill 1h–overnight.
  2. Room temp 10–15 min; cook on medium‑high 2–3 min per side.
  3. Add veg in last minute; garnish with sesame and scallion.

Notes

  • Pear tenderises; apple OK.
  • Keep flavours traditional (no dark soy/oyster).

Storage & Reheating

  • Marinated raw: 24h (fridge) / 1 month (freeze). Cooked: 2–3 days (fridge).

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with rice, lettuce wraps, ssamjang, kimchi, banchan.
