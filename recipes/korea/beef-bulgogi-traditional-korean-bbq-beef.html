Beef Bulgogi (Traditional Korean BBQ Beef) 불고기

Korea · Main

Classic home-style bulgogi: thin-sliced beef marinated with soy, Korean pear, garlic, sesame.

Serves: 3–4 · Prep: 15 minutes · Wait: 1 hour (or overnight) · Cook: 6 minutes

Ingredients

Beef & Vegetables

  • 600–800g thinly sliced beef (ribeye, short rib, or sirloin)
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 scallions, green and white parts separated and sliced
  • (Optional) 1 small carrot, julienned or thinly sliced
  • (Optional) 100g mushrooms (e.g. shiitake), thinly sliced
  • Lettuce or perilla leaves, to serve

Marinade

  • 6 tbsp regular soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar or honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp mirin or 1 tbsp makgeolli (optional)
  • 4 tbsp grated Korean pear (or Fuji apple)
  • 2 tbsp grated onion
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix marinade until sugar dissolves; coat beef well and chill 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Bring to room temp 10–15 minutes. Heat pan/grill to medium-high.
  3. Cook beef in batches 2–3 minutes per side until caramelised. Add vegetables in last minute if using.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and scallion greens. Serve with rice, lettuce wraps, and ssamjang.

Notes

  • Korean pear tenderises; a sweet apple works in a pinch.
  • Avoid dark soy/oyster sauce to keep traditional flavour.
  • Soak glass noodles before adding, if using.

Storage & Reheating

  • Marinated beef: fridge 24h or freeze 1 month. Cooked bulgogi: fridge 2–3 days.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with rice, lettuce/perilla leaves, ssamjang, kimchi, and banchan.
