Country: Korea | Type: Main

Thinly sliced beef marinated in a mixture of soy, garlic and sugar, then grilled or pan-cooked.

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef (ribeye), thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp rice wine or mirin
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 pear or kiwi, grated
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • Sesame seeds

Method

  1. Mix soy sauce, sugar, rice wine, garlic, sesame oil and grated fruit to make a marinade.
  2. Marinate the beef and sliced onion in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cook in a hot pan or grill until the beef is cooked through and slightly caramelized.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve with rice.
