Doenjang Jjigae

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Korea | Type: Soup

A savoury soybean paste stew with tofu and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp doenjang (soybean paste)
  • 2 cups anchovy broth
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 potato, cubed
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 block tofu, cubed
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, sliced (optional)

Method

  1. Dissolve the doenjang in boiling anchovy broth.
  2. Add zucchini, potato and onion; simmer until vegetables are tender.
  3. Add tofu and garlic; cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Add sliced chili if desired; serve hot with rice.
