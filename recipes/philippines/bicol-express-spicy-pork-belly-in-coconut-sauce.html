Bicol Express (Spicy Pork Belly in Coconut Sauce)

Philippines • Main • Serves 4 • Prep 15 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 40 minutes

Rich coconut‑chilli pork with sautéed shrimp paste — Bicolano favourite.

Ingredients

Pork Belly

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 500g pork belly, 1cm strips

Aromatics

  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 5cm ginger, grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped

Sauce

  • 65g sautéed shrimp paste (bagoong guisado)
  • 750ml coconut milk + extra

Chillies

  • 6 long green chillies, sliced
  • 2 long red chillies, sliced
  • 2 bird’s‑eye chillies, sliced

To Serve

  • Steamed rice

Instructions

  1. Brown pork in batches.
  2. Sauté garlic/ginger 1 min; onion 4 min.
  3. Return pork; add bagoong 1 min.
  4. Add coconut milk; simmer 10 min.
  5. Add chillies; simmer ~12 min until tender/thickened. Serve.

Notes

  • Reduce bird’s‑eye chillies for milder heat.
  • Pork shoulder for leaner option.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; reheat gently (avoid boiling).

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with garlic kangkong or bok choy; green mango salad.
