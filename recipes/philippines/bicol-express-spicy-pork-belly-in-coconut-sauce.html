Bicol Express (Spicy Pork Belly in Coconut Sauce)

Philippines · Main

Rich, spicy coconut pork with shrimp paste and chillies — a Bicol classic.

Serves: 4 · Prep: 15 minutes · Wait: 0 minutes · Cook: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Pork Belly

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 500g pork belly, skinless, 1cm strips

Aromatics

  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 5cm ginger, finely grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped

Sauce

  • 65g sautéed shrimp paste (bagoong guisado)
  • 750ml coconut milk, plus extra to drizzle

Chillies

  • 6 long green chillies, seeded and sliced
  • 2 long red chillies, seeded and sliced
  • 2 red bird’s‑eye chillies, sliced

To Serve

  • Steamed rice

Instructions

  1. Brown pork in batches; set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic and ginger 1 min; add onion 4 min.
  3. Return pork; add shrimp paste 1 min.
  4. Pour coconut milk; simmer 10 min.
  5. Add chillies; simmer ~12 min until tender and sauce thickened. Garnish and serve.

Notes

  • Adjust heat by reducing bird’s‑eye chillies.
  • Pork shoulder can substitute for a leaner version.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; reheat gently (avoid boiling) with splash coconut milk/water.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with garlic kangkong or bok choy; green mango salad for contrast.
