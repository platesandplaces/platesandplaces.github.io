Binagoongang Baboy – Modern Version (Pork Belly with Guisado Bagoong)

Philippines • Main • Serves 3–4 • Prep 20 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 1 hour 30 minutes

Crispy double‑cooked pork belly in tomato‑bagoong sauce with fresh chillies.

Ingredients

For the pork

  • 600g pork belly, skin on
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 6 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp fine salt
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Chilli leaves (optional)
  • Steamed rice

For the guisado bagoong

  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 60ml oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp sautéed shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 1 long green chilli
  • 2 bird’s‑eye chillies, sliced

Instructions

  1. Par‑cook pork ~1h with aromatics; cool and cut.
  2. Deep‑fry at 180°C ~3 min; drain (and fry chilli leaves briefly if using).
  3. Caramelise tomatoes with onion/garlic; add bagoong.
  4. Add a little reserved broth; add pork+chillies; simmer 3–4 min. Serve.

Notes

  • Use ripe tomatoes; cook raw bagoong first if using.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; avoid freezing (texture).

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with jasmine rice; add grilled eggplant or green mango salad.
