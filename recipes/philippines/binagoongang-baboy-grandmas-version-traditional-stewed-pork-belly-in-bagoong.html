Binagoongang Baboy – Grandma’s Version (Traditional Stewed Pork Belly in Bagoong)

Philippines • Main • Serves 3–4 • Prep 10 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 1 hour

Comforting stewed pork belly gently simmered with shrimp paste and vinegar.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 600g pork belly, chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp sautéed shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 long green chilli (optional)
  • 1 cup water
  • Steamed rice

Instructions

  1. Render/brown pork; reserve 1–2 tbsp fat.
  2. Sauté garlic/onion; add bagoong 2–3 min.
  3. Add vinegar; boil 1 min without stirring; then sugar (opt) and water (+ chilli if using).
  4. Cover; simmer low 45–60 min until tender/thick. Serve with rice.

Notes

  • Boil vinegar briefly to remove sharpness.
  • Low heat ensures tenderness and melded flavour.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 4 days; freeze 1 month.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with eggplant/okra; pair with sinigang.
