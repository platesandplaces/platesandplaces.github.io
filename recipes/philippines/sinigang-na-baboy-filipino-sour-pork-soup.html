Sinigang na Baboy (Filipino Sour Pork Soup)

Philippines • Soup • Serves 4–6 • Prep 20 minutes • Wait 15 minutes (tamarind soak) • Cook 45 minutes

Tangy, savoury pork soup with tamarind — balanced and comforting.

Ingredients

For the soup

  • 60 ml vegetable oil
  • 500 g pork ribs
  • 500 g pork belly, 4 cm pieces
  • 1 onion, wedges
  • 3 tomatoes, wedges
  • 1 red capsicum, wedges
  • 1.5 L water
  • 4 cm horseradish, pieces
  • 1 Japanese eggplant, sliced
  • 200 g snake/green beans
  • 2 tsp salt; black pepper

For the souring agent

  • 100 g tamarind pulp
  • 250 ml boiling water

To serve

  • Sliced spring onions; steamed rice; fish sauce; kalamansi/lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Brown ribs and belly; set aside.
  2. Sauté onion, tomatoes, capsicum; return pork + water; simmer 15 min.
  3. Soak tamarind 15 min; strain; add to pot.
  4. Add horseradish, eggplant; 5 min. Add beans; 3 min.
  5. Season; adjust sour/salt; serve with rice.

Notes

  • Bone‑in adds flavour; tamarind concentrate can sub (2–3 tbsp).

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; reheat gently.

Serving Suggestions

  • Great next day; thin with water if needed.
