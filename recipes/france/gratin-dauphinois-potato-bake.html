Gratin Dauphinois (Potato Bake)

France · Side

Classic creamy potato bake with milk, cream, nutmeg — simple and crowd‑pleasing.

Serves: 6 (side) · Prep: 15 minutes · Cook: 55 minutes

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 kg waxy potatoes
  • 400 ml milk
  • 300 ml double cream
  • Nutmeg
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Butter (for dish)

Instructions

  1. Simmer potato slices with milk, cream, mustard, salt, nutmeg.
  2. Rub dish with garlic; butter base.
  3. Bake 200°C for 40–45 min. Rest, garnish optional herbs.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; reheat in oven or microwave.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with roast meats or green salad.
