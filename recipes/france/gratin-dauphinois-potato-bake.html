Gratin Dauphinois (Potato Bake)

France • Side • Serves 6 (side) • Prep 15 minutes • Wait 0 minutes • Cook 55 minutes

Classic creamy potato bake with milk, cream, nutmeg.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 kg waxy potatoes
  • 400 ml milk
  • 300 ml double cream
  • Nutmeg
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Butter (for dish)

Instructions

  1. Simmer potato slices with milk, cream, mustard, salt, nutmeg.
  2. Rub dish with garlic; butter base.
  3. Bake 200°C 40–45 min; rest before serving.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge 3 days; reheat oven/microwave.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with roast meats or a green salad.
