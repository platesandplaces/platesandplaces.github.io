Dorayaki

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Japan | Type: Dessert

Dorayaki are fluffy pancakes filled with sweet red bean paste, a beloved Japanese treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 70 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 200 g sweet red bean paste (anko)

Method

  1. Beat the eggs with sugar and honey until frothy.
  2. Sift the flour and baking powder into the egg mixture and mix to form a smooth batter.
  3. Cook small pancakes on a lightly oiled pan until bubbles form; flip and cook until golden.
  4. Sandwich a spoonful of red bean paste between two pancakes.
