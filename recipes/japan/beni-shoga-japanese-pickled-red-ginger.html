Beni Shoga (Japanese Pickled Red Ginger) 紅生姜

Japan • Condiment • Serves ~200g jar • Prep 10 minutes • Wait 3–5 days (pickling) • Cook 0 minutes

Punchy red ginger pickled in plum vinegar; classic garnish.

Ingredients

For the ginger

  • 100g ginger (young preferred)
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Pickling liquid

  • 100 ml ume vinegar (umezu)
  • Optional: red shiso leaves

Instructions

  1. Julienne ginger; salt 30 min; rinse/dry.
  2. Pack in sterilised jar; cover fully with ume vinegar (+ shiso).
  3. Refrigerate 3–5 days; keep submerged.

Notes

  • Young ginger = milder; colour comes from ume vinegar.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 1 month.

Serving Suggestions

  • Garnish for yakisoba/okonomiyaki/gyudon/katsu curry.
