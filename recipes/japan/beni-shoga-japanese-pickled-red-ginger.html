Beni Shoga (Japanese Pickled Red Ginger) 紅生姜

Japan · Condiment

Punchy red ginger pickled in plum vinegar — classic garnish for okonomiyaki, yakisoba, gyudon.

Serves: ~200g jar · Prep: 10 minutes · Wait: 3–5 days (pickling) · Cook: 0 minutes

Ingredients

For the ginger

  • 100g ginger (young preferred)
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Pickling liquid

  • 100 ml ume plum vinegar (umezu)
  • Optional: a few red shiso leaves

Instructions

  1. Julienne ginger; salt 30 minutes to draw moisture. Rinse and pat dry.
  2. Pack in sterilised jar; cover fully with ume vinegar (+ shiso if using).
  3. Refrigerate 3–5 days before using; keep submerged.

Notes

  • Young ginger = milder; mature ginger is sharper but fine.
  • Colour comes naturally from ume vinegar; no dye needed.

Storage & Reheating

  • Fridge up to 1 month when submerged.

Serving Suggestions

  • Garnish for rich/savoury dishes like yakisoba, gyudon, katsu curry.
