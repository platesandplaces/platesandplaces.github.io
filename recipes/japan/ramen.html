Country: Japan | Type: Soup

A classic Japanese noodle soup with a soy-flavoured broth, ramen noodles and traditional toppings.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin (optional)
  • Ramen noodles
  • Sliced pork belly (chashu)
  • Soft-boiled eggs
  • Nori sheets
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Green onions, chopped

Method

  1. Heat the broth and add soy sauce (and mirin, if using).
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions and drain.
  3. Divide noodles among bowls and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with sliced pork, halved eggs, nori, bamboo shoots and green onions.
