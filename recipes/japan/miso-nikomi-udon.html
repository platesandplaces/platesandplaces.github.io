Miso Nikomi Udon

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Japan | Type: Main

A Nagoya specialty, this miso-based hot pot features udon noodles simmered in a savory broth.

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • 2 tbsp red miso paste
  • 2 cups dashi stock
  • 1 chicken thigh, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • Optional: tofu, fish cake

Method

  1. Bring dashi to a simmer and dissolve the miso paste.
  2. Add chicken and mushrooms; simmer until cooked.
  3. Add udon noodles and cook until tender.
  4. Crack eggs into the broth and cook to desired doneness.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
