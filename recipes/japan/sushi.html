Country: Japan | Type: Main

Learn to make simple sushi rolls (maki) with vinegared rice, nori and your favourite fillings.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice, cooked and seasoned
  • Nori sheets
  • Fillings: cucumber, avocado, crab sticks or cooked seafood
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger

Method

  1. Spread a thin layer of rice on a nori sheet, leaving a border at the top.
  2. Place the fillings along the bottom edge of the rice.
  3. Roll up tightly using a bamboo sushi mat.
  4. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
  5. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi and pickled ginger.
