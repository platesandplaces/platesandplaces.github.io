Katsu Curry

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Japan | Type: Main

Katsu curry is a comforting dish consisting of a crispy breaded pork cutlet served with a rich curry sauce over rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • 1 breaded pork cutlet (tonkatsu)
  • 1 packet Japanese curry roux
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Method

  1. Prepare the rice according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pork cutlet until golden and crisp; slice into strips.
  3. Saut√© the onion, carrot, and potato; add water and curry roux to make sauce.
  4. Serve the curry over the rice and top with sliced cutlet.
