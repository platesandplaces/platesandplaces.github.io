Tamagoyaki

Home Recipes Travel Food Guide About

Country: Japan | Type: Side

Tamagoyaki is a slightly sweet rolled omelette often enjoyed in bento boxes or as a sushi topping.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Oil for pan

Method

  1. Beat the eggs with sugar, mirin and soy sauce until well combined.
  2. Heat a small square or rectangular pan and lightly oil it.
  3. Pour a thin layer of egg mixture; when set, roll it to one side.
  4. Add more egg mixture and continue rolling until all the egg is used.
  5. Allow to cool slightly, then slice and serve.
