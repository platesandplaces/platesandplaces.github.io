<div class="recipe-meta">
  <strong>Serves:</strong> 3–4 (side)<br>
  <strong>Prep:</strong> 10 minutes<br>
  <strong>Cook:</strong> 15–20 minutes<br>
</div>

<h4>Ingredients</h4>
<ul>
<li>300 g kabocha, peeled and chunked</li>
<li>2 tbsp Japanese mayonnaise</li>
<li>1 tsp rice vinegar</li>
<li>½ tsp sugar</li>
<li>Pinch salt & black pepper</li>
</ul>

<h4>Instructions</h4>
<ol>
<li>Steam/boil kabocha until just tender; drain well.</li>
<li>Lightly mash; fold in mayo, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper.</li>
<li>Serve warm, room temp or chilled.</li>
</ol>

<h4>Notes</h4>
<ul><li>For firmer texture, fold in 1–2 tbsp panko.</li></ul>

<h4>Storage</h4>
<ul><li>Refrigerate up to 2 days. Do not reheat.</li></ul>
