About Plates & Places

Welcome! Plates & Places is a personal project capturing the recipes I love, stories from the road, and a curated food guide. It’s a growing collection, built slowly with care and curiosity.

Who am I?

I’m a food enthusiast and occasional traveler, fascinated by the way food and place shape one another. I cook at home, eat out often, and jot down tips so I don’t forget my favorite bites or hidden gems.

What you’ll find here

  • Recipes I actually cook, mostly traditional, sometimes tweaked. Organized by country.
  • Travel stories from trips I’ve taken, focusing on practical tips and memorable experiences.
  • A food guide with honest notes about restaurants, cafés, markets and street food that I recommend.

This site is always evolving. If something is missing, it probably means I haven’t written it yet.
